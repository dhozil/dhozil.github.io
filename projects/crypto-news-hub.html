Crypto News Hub - AI-Powered Crypto News Platform

Hackathon Project • Tags: AI GenLayer News

Crypto News Hub is an intelligent news aggregation platform that automatically fetches and curates crypto news using AI, allows community members to submit and vote on content, and rewards quality contributions with on-chain tokens using GenLayer Intelligent Contracts for content validation.

Overview

Crypto News Hub combines AI-powered content curation with community governance and on-chain rewards to create a transparent, censorship-resistant news platform.

Architecture

Frontend (Next.js + TypeScript)

  • React-based web application
  • MetaMask wallet integration
  • Real-time news feed
  • Community posting interface

Smart Contracts (GenLayer)

  • Content validation logic
  • Reward distribution system
  • Governance mechanisms
  • AI-powered quality assessment
